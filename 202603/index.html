From Prompt to Product

Early Lessons Building AI Agents

Yan Xie
Director of Engineering at Webflow, leading Growth Engineering and AI initiatives. Previously led monetization areas at YouTube Ads including Shorts.

Building an AI agent is easy in a demo, but turning it into a reliable product is much harder. This session shares early lessons and common challenges teams face when moving from prompt experiments to real-world applications.

We'll discuss performance bottlenecks, prioritization trade-offs, and why rigorous evaluation must be part of the development process from day one. We'll also explore how teams balance launch timelines with system reliability to ensure AI initiatives deliver meaningful user value.
